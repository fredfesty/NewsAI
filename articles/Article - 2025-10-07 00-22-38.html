Introduction

What began in 2015 as a celebration of Amazon's 20th anniversary has morphed into a global retail phenomenon 7, 13. Amazon Prime Day, and its subsequent iterations, have reshaped the retail calendar, creating a significant mid-year and now autumn shopping peak that rivals the traditional holiday season sales events like Black Friday and Cyber Monday 3, 7. The event has expanded in duration and global reach, now spanning multiple days and taking place in over 25 countries 12. In 2025, Amazon has continued its trend of holding multiple Prime member-exclusive sales events, with the "Prime Big Deal Days" taking place in October 2, 6, 11. This autumn event provides a crucial opportunity for consumers to begin their holiday shopping early, with substantial discounts offered across a vast array of products 6, 32. The influence of this sales event extends beyond Amazon, creating a ripple effect across the e-commerce industry and prompting other major retailers to offer competing promotions 3, 49.

The Genesis and Evolution of a Retail Juggernaut

The inaugural Prime Day was a 24-hour event on 15 July 2015, designed to offer a volume of deals greater than Black Friday, exclusively for Prime members 13. Its success was immediate, and over the years, the event has expanded significantly. By 2019, it had grown to a 48-hour event, with sales surpassing the previous Black Friday and Cyber Monday combined 13. The COVID-19 pandemic in 2020 necessitated a shift from the usual July date, but the event continued to deliver significant sales, with a particular focus on supporting small and medium-sized businesses 13. Since 2022, Amazon has established a pattern of holding two major sales events for Prime members each year, one in the summer and another in the autumn, now branded as Prime Big Deal Days 16, 36. The 2025 July Prime Day was the longest to date, running for four days from 8th to 11th July 8, 12. The October 2025 event, running from 7th to 8th October, continues this trend of multi-day sales extravaganzas 2, 6, 39. This evolution reflects a strategic effort by Amazon to stimulate consumer spending during traditionally slower retail periods and to continually reinforce the value of its Prime membership program 7, 29.

The Economic Ripple Effect

The economic impact of Amazon's Prime Day events is substantial. The July 2023 sale, for instance, generated over $12.90 billion in global sales 3. The Prime Day events contribute significantly to the economy by driving consumer spending and creating temporary employment in logistics and customer service 3. The influence of Prime Day extends to other retailers, who often launch their own sales events to compete, leading to a broader period of heightened consumer spending 3, 49. This has been observed in the UK, where other retailers often run promotions concurrently with Prime Day 48. The event also provides a significant platform for small and medium-sized businesses that sell on the Amazon Marketplace, with customers spending over $3 billion on items from these businesses during a previous Prime Day 3. The October 2025 Prime Big Deal Days are projected to see a 7% growth in US gross merchandise value compared to the previous year, making it the largest October Prime event to date 49.

Navigating the Deals: Technology

The technology sector consistently sees some of the most sought-after deals during Prime Day events, and October 2025 is no exception. Apple products, which are rarely discounted, are a major focus for many shoppers. This year, significant savings can be found on various models of the iPad and AirPods 5, 21, 40, 41, 42. For instance, the 11-inch M3 iPad Air has seen price reductions of around $150, while the AirPods 4 have been available for approximately $40 off their usual price 5, 21. The AirPods Pro 2 have also been heavily discounted, with savings of around $80 5, 21.

Beyond Apple, Amazon's own devices, such as the Fire TV Stick and Echo speakers, are typically offered at substantial discounts 12, 30. Headphones from other major brands, TVs, and other home electronics are also featured prominently in the sale 39. The event provides an opportunity for consumers to upgrade their personal and home technology at a fraction of the usual cost.

Indulgence and Innovation: Beauty and Personal Care

The beauty and personal care category has become a significant component of Amazon's Prime Day sales. High-end and highly coveted items like the Dyson Airwrap multi-styler are often included in the promotions, with rare discounts of around Â£100 being offered 14, 43, 44, 47, 50. The Oral-B iO series, for example, has seen significant price reductions, providing consumers with access to advanced dental care technology at a lower cost 20, 34. Beyond these hero products, a wide range of skincare and makeup from both budget-friendly and premium brands are also included in the sale, with discounts on popular brands like CeraVe, Elemis, and Maybelline 9, 19, 24, 25, 28.

Consumer Behaviour and Strategic Shopping

The timing of the October Prime Big Deal Days is strategically placed to capture early holiday shopping demand 6. Many consumers now wait for these sales events to make significant purchases, having been conditioned to expect major discounts in the middle of the year and in the autumn 3. This has led to a shift in shopping patterns, with a notable increase in online spending during these periods 46. During the July 2025 Prime Day, for example, US consumers spent an estimated $24.1 billion online over the four-day event 46.

For consumers, navigating the sheer volume of deals can be a challenge. It is advisable for shoppers to have a clear idea of what they are looking for and to research prices in advance to ensure they are getting a genuine bargain. While the discounts can be substantial, not all deals are created equal, and some may be on older models or less desirable products. The event is exclusively for Amazon Prime members, which serves as a powerful incentive for consumers to subscribe to the service 32.

Conclusion

Amazon's Prime Big Deal Days in October 2025 underscore the continued evolution of the retail landscape. What started as a one-off anniversary celebration has become a biannual, multi-day global shopping event that dictates consumer spending habits and influences the strategies of competing retailers. The significant discounts on high-demand products across technology, home appliances, and beauty provide tangible benefits to consumers, particularly in the lead-up to the festive season. As this retail phenomenon continues to grow and adapt, its impact on the broader economy and the way in which we shop is likely to become even more pronounced.
